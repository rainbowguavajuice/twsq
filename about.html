twsq is a collection of quotes taken from conversations that happened over the internet: mostly IRC, and later Discord. Some are attributed to fictional characters. The content was initially hosted on tumblr, before it was migrated to these pages in early-mid 2020. Updates are intermittent, and happen whenever someone nearby says something amusing in or out of context.

All quotes listed here are with the knowledge of the speakers. If you would no longer like your words to appear on these pages, or if you would like them attributed to a name other than your handle at the time the words were spoken, please contact me.
